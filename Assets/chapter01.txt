Story about cool fantasy stuff that makes you go like "oh wow, this is pretty cool".

Many heroes are spread across the various lands. The *bad thing that's happening* is plaguing the world and it must be stopped. From the humble village of Mediocris to the great city structures of Dryadalis, the lives of many creatures hang by only a thread.

It is up to... well many people actually.. to fix this mess. Who stands up to the challenge?

That is up to you to decide.
